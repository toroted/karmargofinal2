Terms and Policies

Your membership at Karm Agro Farm is based on the following terms and conditions. By becoming a member, you agree to these terms, which are intended to create a safe and harmonious community for everyone.

Membership Terms & Conditions

  • Family Membership: A single membership will be considered for a family of a maximum of six people.
  • Membership Duration: The membership period for each family will be 10 years from the date of enrollment.
  • Trial Period: The membership is binding for the first year. If, after one year, you are not satisfied with the services or facilities, you can cancel your membership. Your payment will be refunded with 14% annual interest via check within one month.
  • Non-Refundable Status: After the first year, your family will be considered a permanent member, and the membership cannot be canceled for the remaining nine years.
  • Daily Visits: Members can visit the farmhouse for a total of 60 days per year. This period is calculated from January 1st to December 31st.
  • Bonuses: Members who visit for the full 60 days in a year will receive a bonus of ₹7,000 at the end of the year. If you use fewer than 60 days, you will be paid ₹200 for each unused day.
  • No Visit Bonus: If a family does not visit the farmhouse even once during a year, they will receive a check for ₹25,000 at the end of that year.
  • Code of Conduct: Any antisocial behavior will not be tolerated. The farm management reserves the right to cancel the membership of any family engaging in such behavior.
  • No Legal Rights: Membership only provides access to the facilities and does not grant any legal ownership rights to the property or its assets.
